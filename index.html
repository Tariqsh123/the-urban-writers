We offer editing, design, typesetting, publishing, and marketing solutions tailored for authors to bring their books to life professionally.

Most projects take between 4–12 weeks depending on the service package and author feedback speed.

Yes. You retain 100% ownership of your work. The Urban Writers simply helps you prepare and publish it professionally.

Absolutely! Our design team collaborates with you to create a cover that reflects your story and appeals to your target readers.
